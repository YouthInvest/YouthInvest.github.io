Our Programs & Services

Engaging, hands-on programs that make financial literacy fun and accessible for kids

1

Workshops

In-Vest workshops teach children essential skills for managing money and understanding investing. Each workshop covers a different topic, using fun and interactive slides to keep kids engaged while building practical financial knowledge.

At In-Vest, we run fun, hands-on workshops where kids learn about saving, budgeting, and even investing. Through games and real-life activities, they get to practice money skills in a way that's easy to understand and exciting, helping them feel confident about handling money now and in the future.

Interactive slides and activities
Age-appropriate financial concepts
Real-world money scenarios
Fun games and simulations

Guest Speakers

In-Vest invites finance professionals and experts to speak with children, sharing real-world experiences and advice. These sessions inspire students, show them practical applications of money skills and help them see the possibilities for their financial future.

Our guest speakers come from various financial backgrounds including banking, investment, entrepreneurship, and financial planning. They share their personal journeys and provide valuable insights that help students understand the real-world applications of financial literacy.

Finance professionals from various fields
Real-world career insights
Q&A sessions with experts
Inspirational success stories

3

Hands-On Projects

In-Vest gives children the chance to practice what they learn through activities like small presentations and our unique Stock Market Game simulation. These projects let students apply financial concepts in a fun, interactive way while building confidence and real-world skills.

Our hands-on projects are designed to reinforce learning through practical application. Students work on real-life financial scenarios, create budgets, make investment decisions, and present their findings, developing both financial literacy and essential life skills.

Stock Market Game simulation
Budgeting and saving challenges
Presentation and public speaking
Team-based financial projects

Marketing & Outreach

How we reach students and families

School Partnerships

We work with local elementary and middle schools to offer workshops as part of after-school programs or special events. This lets parents and teachers see firsthand how valuable the program is, while giving kids a fun, hands-on way to learn about money.

Media & Parent Networks

We use Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook to share short, fun videos of our workshops, student success stories, and helpful money tips. We also reach families through parent groups, PTA newsletters, and community forums.

Community Partnerships

We run free demos at libraries, community centers, and local events so kids and parents can see how fun and hands-on our workshops are. We also do mini-sessions at summer camps and after-school programs.
