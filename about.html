About Youth In-Vest

Learn our story, mission, and vision for empowering youth through financial literacy

Who We Are

Youth In-Vest is a nonprofit that helps kids learn how to save, spend, and grow money. Through fun workshops and activities, we teach personal financial literacy and the basics of investing in a way that's easy to understand.

We want kids to start investing early and prepare for their future with confidence. Based in Cupertino, CA and Houston, TX.

Our Target Market

Primary

Parents who want their children to learn about money and start building financial skills early

Secondary

Elementary and middle school children starting to learn about money

Our Mission

At In-Vest, we believe every child deserves the chance to understand and take control of their money. Our mission is to teach kids personal finance and investing in a way that's engaging, empowering, and practical so they can grow up confident, prepared, and capable of making smart financial choices for their future.

Our Vision

We imagine a future where every child understands money and feels confident making smart choices. By teaching financial literacy and investing early, we hope kids can grow up prepared, independent, and ready to create the life they dream of.

Our Method

We bring learning to life through interactive workshops, mentorship programs, and hands-on projects that go beyond the classroom. Market Analysis

Understanding the need for financial education

There is strong demand for financial education among young students and their parents. 85% of parents worry about passing on poor money habits, and over 76% believe children should learn about finances before high school. Many elementary and middle school kids show interest in learning money skills, making In-Vest likely to succeed.
