Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Youth In-Vest programs?

Our programs are designed for elementary and middle school students. We also have volunteer opportunities for high school students (8th-11th graders) to join our team.

How much do your programs cost?

Most of our workshops and programs are free or low-cost. We believe financial education should be accessible to all students.

How can my school partner with Youth In-Vest?

We partner with schools through after-school programs, special events, and workshops. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities for your school.

Do you offer virtual programs?

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual workshops to accommodate different needs and locations.
